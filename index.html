Independent Coastal Property Assessments for Asset Protection, Risk Clarity, and Maintenance Planning

About Us

Coastal Asset Solutions provides independent assessment services for coastal property owners who need clarity on asset condition, corrosion risk, and maintenance priorities. Our role is to act as a neutral technical advisor between property owners and contractors.

With hands-on exposure to coastal environments, structural wear patterns, and maintenance workflows, our focus is on identifying what is actually happening to your asset — not what is being sold to you.

The Problem We Solve

In coastal environments, deterioration is often hidden until damage becomes expensive. Contractors are typically engaged to repair, not to independently assess — which can lead to:

  • Over-scoped or duplicated work
  • Missed early-stage corrosion
  • Lack of prioritisation across multiple issues
  • Unclear or inconsistent quotes

Our Role — The Independent Layer

We sit between the asset owner and contractors as an independent review layer. Our objective is to ensure decisions are based on asset condition, not commercial bias.

The Toolbox Approach

We use structured assessment frameworks (“toolboxes”) depending on the situation:

  • Exposure Mapping Toolbox — identifies environmental stress zones such as salt exposure, prevailing weather, drainage issues, and water ingress pathways
  • Corrosion Detection Toolbox — identifies early-stage degradation before visible failure becomes expensive
  • Scope Validation Toolbox — ensures contractor work is correctly defined, complete, and aligned to the actual problem
  • Quote Integrity Toolbox — identifies inconsistencies, missing items, duplicated scope, or inflated work
  • Lifecycle Planning Toolbox — helps owners prioritise long-term maintenance spending across coastal assets

Core Services

Coastal Property Snapshot™

A structured overview of visible condition, exposure risk zones, and early-stage deterioration indicators. This service is designed for owners who want an independent first look at the property before deciding on maintenance, remediation, or contractor engagement.

Coastal Asset Protection Review™

A deeper technical assessment of corrosion risk, material vulnerability, and environmental exposure pathways. This is suited to owners who need a clearer understanding of how the coastal environment is affecting long-term asset performance and what should be prioritised.

Independent Scope Development™

A contractor-ready scope definition designed to make sure all parties are pricing the same clearly defined work. This helps reduce ambiguity, avoid under-scoped quotes, and keep repair recommendations tied to the actual asset condition.

Independent Quote Review™

A technical review of contractor pricing and scope alignment to identify missing work, duplicated work, unnecessary inclusions, or scope mismatches. The goal is to keep contractors honest and help owners make better-informed decisions before committing to spend.

Annual Coastal Asset Review™

A recurring assessment tracking asset condition over time and updating maintenance priorities as conditions change. This service is designed for owners who want to protect high-value coastal property through planned, staged maintenance rather than reactive repairs.

Why Independence Matters

Coastal assets degrade continuously due to environmental exposure. Without independent review, decisions are often influenced by contractors whose incentives are aligned with repair volume, not asset optimisation.

Our role is to keep the focus on the asset itself — its condition, its risks, and its long-term performance.
