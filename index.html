Coastal Asset Solutions Ltd

Independent Coastal Property Assessments for Long-Term Asset Protection

What We Do

We provide independent, non-biased coastal property assessments focused on identifying risk, asset condition, and maintenance priorities. Our role is advisory only — not repair or contracting work.

Core Services

Coastal Property Snapshot™

A high-level assessment identifying visible corrosion risks, exposure issues, drainage problems, and early warning signs of deterioration.

Coastal Asset Protection Review™

A deeper analysis of environmental exposure and long-term asset risk, with prioritised maintenance guidance.

Independent Scope Development™

A structured scope document to ensure maintenance or repair work is clearly defined before contractors provide pricing.

Independent Quote Review™

A review of contractor quotes to identify inconsistencies, unnecessary work, or missing scope items.

Annual Coastal Asset Review™

A yearly check-up tracking condition changes and helping plan long-term maintenance strategy.

Why Independence Matters

Coastal environments accelerate corrosion and structural degradation. Independent assessment ensures decisions are not influenced by repair incentives or contractor bias.
