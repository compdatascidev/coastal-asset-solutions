Independent Coastal Property Condition & Risk Assessments

Clear, Independent Insight for Coastal Property Owners

We provide independent assessments of coastal property condition, focusing on corrosion risk, structural exposure, and long-term maintenance priorities. We do not perform repairs or sell remediation work — our role is purely advisory and independent.

Why This Matters

Coastal environments accelerate corrosion and hidden structural degradation. Without independent assessment, property owners often rely on contractor-driven advice that can overstate or misprioritise work.

What We Help You Understand

  • What is actually deteriorating vs what is cosmetic
  • Which issues are urgent vs long-term maintenance
  • Where corrosion risk is developing (before it becomes visible)
  • Whether contractor quotes are complete and fair
  • How to prioritise maintenance spending effectively
