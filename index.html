Independent Insight for Coastal Property Owners

We provide independent assessments focused on corrosion risk, structural exposure, and long-term maintenance prioritisation. We do not perform repairs or contracting work — our role is purely advisory.

Before You Contact Us

To ensure we provide the right level of assessment, please understand there are different types of service depending on your situation.

  • Snapshot Assessment — visual condition overview and early risk identification
  • Protection Review — deeper analysis of exposure, corrosion risk, and deterioration patterns
  • Scope Development — structured plan for contractors before work begins
  • Quote Review — independent review of contractor pricing and scope accuracy

What We Help You Avoid

  • Unnecessary or duplicated contractor work
  • Missed early corrosion indicators
  • Unclear or incomplete maintenance scopes
  • Overpaying due to undefined project scope

Who This Is For

Property owners who want clarity before committing to maintenance, renovation, or remediation work in coastal environments.
